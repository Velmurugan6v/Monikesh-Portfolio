🕹️ Escape Room: Hidden Riddles

👩‍💻 Role: Game Designer, Level Designer, Narrator & Puzzle Designer

🛠️ Tools Used: Unity

📜 About the Game

Hidden Riddles is an 🏺 ancient-themed fantasy adventure packed with mystery and twists. Twin princesses, separated and raised in very different worlds, reunite to uncover the truth behind their cursed kingdom and reclaim their destiny. Players explore magical lands, uncover secrets, and restore balance to their world.

🎮 Storyline & Gameplay

Players travel through vibrant ✨ fantasy environments like Frog Village 🐸, Mushroom Village 🍄, Carnival 🎡 and mystical tribal forests 🌳. They solve ancient puzzles, recover magical stones, and face their cousin’s dark power while discovering their own hidden heritage.

🕹️ Game Mechanics

  • 🧩 Interactive puzzles & riddles: Solve ancient symbols, rotating stone mechanisms, and memory-based tasks to progress.
  • 🐉 Mini-games: Engage in thrilling dragon fights 🐲, clever frog challenges 🐸, and adventurous boat rides 🚤 that break up the main storyline.
  • 🎒 Exploration & inventory: Collect enchanted items ✨ and use them to unlock portals or reveal hidden paths.
  • 💬 Dialogue-driven choices: Interactions with tribal leaders, magical beings, and allies affect story direction 🗝️.
  • 🌍 Fantasy environments: Each level offers distinct visuals and mechanics tied to its setting, immersing players in a living, ancient world.
