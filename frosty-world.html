🎄 Christmas Game: Frosty World

👩‍💻 Role: Game Designer, Level Designer, Narrator, & Puzzle Designer

🛠️ Tools Used: Unity

📜 About the Game

Frosty World is a 🎅 Christmas-themed escape adventure where magic takes a dark turn. A mysterious spell transforms Santa’s once-friendly toys into dangerous foes. Four cousins two 👧 girls and two 👦 boys spending their holidays with their grandfather embark on a quest to reverse the spell and save Christmas.

🎮 Storyline & Gameplay

Players journey through enchanting ❄️ fantasy realms, from snow-laden villages to hidden toy workshops. Along the way, they meet Santa’s allies 🦌🧝, solving puzzles and uncovering clues to break the curse. Each level combines festive visuals with interactive mechanics, creating a playful yet suspenseful Christmas adventure.

🕹️ Game Mechanics

  • 🧩 Puzzle-based progression: Solve riddles, symbol-matching challenges, and hidden-object tasks.
  • 🎒 Inventory & item combination: Collect magical artifacts ✨, combine them to dispel spells or activate mechanisms.
  • 👀 Stealth & timing: Avoid cursed toys 🚂 patrolling zones; timing and movement choices affect success.
  • 💬 Dialogue & choices: Conversations with Santa’s allies reveal clues 🗝️ or alternative paths.
  • 🎭 Mini-boss encounters: Overcome cursed toy guardians through logic-based challenges instead of combat.
